## Research

**Research impact:** I lead [Self-Play SWE-RL (SSR)](https://x.com/YuxiangWei9/status/2003541373853524347?s=20), [SWE-RL](https://github.com/facebookresearch/swe-rl) (NeurIPS'25), [Magicoder](https://github.com/ise-uiuc/magicoder) (ICML'24), and [SelfCodeAlign](https://github.com/bigcode-project/selfcodealign) (NeurIPS'24), projects that have captured broad attention and are widely adopted across industry and open source.

**New:** We released [Self-Play SWE-RL (SSR)](https://x.com/YuxiangWei9/status/2003541373853524347?s=20), which trains a single LLM agent to self-improve by self-playing between bug-injection and bug-repair, grounded in real-world repositories, no human-labeled issues or tests.
